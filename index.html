ABOUT THE CAFE

Established in 2021, Haneul Cafe was founded by three friends who have a passion in baking and loves coffee. Our cafe serves various types of cakes and desserts to satisfy the customers cravings. Located at a small town in Kuala Lumpur, Haneul Cafe ensures beautiful and delicious coffee and desserts.

In addition to our full espresso and brew bar menu, we serve fresh made-to-order breakfast or brunch sandwiches, as well as a selection of any kinds of pastries from croissants, puff pastry and even cinnamon rolls.

"This cafe runs on love, laughter and lots of strong coffee"

Opening hours: everyday from 7am to 9pm

Address: 17 Aciel Street, Kuala Lumpur
